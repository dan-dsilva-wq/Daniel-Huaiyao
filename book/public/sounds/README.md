# Sound Effects

Add the following sound files to this directory:

1. **page-flip.mp3** - A soft page turning sound
2. **submit.mp3** - A gentle chime for when a sentence is submitted
3. **notification.mp3** - A notification sound for when it's your turn

## Free Sound Resources

You can find royalty-free sounds at:
- https://freesound.org/
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- https://www.zapsplat.com/

## Recommended Sounds

For the romantic book theme, look for:
- Soft paper rustling sounds
- Gentle bell or chime sounds
- Harp or piano notification sounds
